Clinical Overview AIH Protocol

Autoimmune Hepatitis (AIH) is a chronic, immune-mediated liver disease requiring precise multi-parameter evaluation. This platform integrates serological markers, histological data, and clinical findings into a structured IAIHG scoring pipeline.

Diagnostic Criteria
  • ● ANA / ASMA / Anti-LKM1
  • ● IgG Serum Levels
  • ● Liver Histology
  • ● Viral Hepatitis Exclusion
Output Deliverables
  • ● IAIHG Score
  • ● Diagnostic Classification
  • ● AI Narrative Summary
  • ● PDF Clinical Report
